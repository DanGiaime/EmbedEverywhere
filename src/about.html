What is this

A simple tool to bring your Twitch stream to other platforms and tools. Make it easier for people to watch your stream wherever they are.

Directly linking to your Twitch stream in a tweet is fine, but did you know you can embed the stream directly inside of Twitter? In order to do this, you need to use a bit of specific HTML.

Why use this?

You can build it that HTML yourself, but then you would need to find a place to host that. Instead, you can log in with Twitch here and have it all done for you!
